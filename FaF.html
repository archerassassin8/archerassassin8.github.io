How to play: 

  • Mouse to aim
  • A and D to controll Roll
  • W and S to control Speed
  • E when reticle is Red to interact with Red objects. Ex: switches or throwable boxes
  • Space to cut engine
  • Hold right click nad move mouse to turn orbit camera
